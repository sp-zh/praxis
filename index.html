Process

A clean structure: Frame → Diverge → Converge → Represent.

01
Frame
Define stakeholders, constraints, success metrics.
02
Diverge
Generate options, explore alternatives, gather evidence.
03
Converge
Decide with tradeoffs, test, iterate, document rationale.
04
Represent
Communicate clearly with visuals, tables, and narrative.
