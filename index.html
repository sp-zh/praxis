Praxis concepts

Scroll inside the window to browse more than 8 concept groups.

Scroll

CTMF

building blocks

Reusable Concepts, Tools, Models, and Frameworks. Each CTMF links to evidence of use and a utility note.

FDCR

process loop

Frame → Diverge → Converge → Represent. A pragmatic loop for evidence-first iteration and clear communication.

Stakeholder map

people

Who is affected, who decides, and what success means for each group.

Requirements & metrics

frame

Translate complaints into testable criteria and measurable success metrics.

Assumption log

risk

List assumptions, their risk level, and how to validate them before high effort builds.

Proxy test

test

Fast, low-fidelity tests to validate key claims early and reduce uncertainty.

Measurement matrix

compare

Compare concepts with numbers such as time, steps, footprint, cleaning, and reliability.

PUGH chart

converge

Structured comparison versus a reference concept to build a defensible selection rationale.

Pairwise comparison

weights

Clarify priorities by comparing criteria pair by pair to derive weights and resolve close calls.

Evidence trail

navigation

Claim → Evidence → So-what. Keep evidence within 10 seconds of each major claim.

Failure modes

engineering

Identify how the system fails before optimizing. Guides safer convergence.

Representation

clarity

Make decisions legible with annotated figures, structured writing, and linked artifacts.

Reflection loop

learning

What worked, what failed, what I would change. Converts projects into reusable practice.
